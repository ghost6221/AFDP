Starting a Community Garden

Starting a community garden, either as a side hobby or for fun, has multiple benefits to your community. This may include:

  • Provide aesthetics to surrounding area and cheap, fresh produce for local neighbors.

  • "Improve soil, water, and air quality though" biodiversity.

  • Help reduce trash in landfills by using food waste as fertilizer.

  • Increases property value.

  • Can provide a center of nutritional and gardening education.

  • Provide yourself with cheap and fresh produce if you decide to make your garden personal.
